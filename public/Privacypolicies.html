Data Protection under GDPR & CCPA Privacy

General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)

The General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) grants you several rights regarding your personal data. As a data controller, the Company is committed to ensuring that these rights are respected and upheld. These rights include

  1. Right of access: You have the right to request a copy of the personal data that the Company holds about you, as well as information about how it is processed
  2. Right to rectification: If you believe that any information the Company holds about you is inaccurate or incomplete, you have the right to request that it be corrected.
  3. Right to object: You have the right to object to the processing of your personal data where the Company is relying on legitimate interest as the legal basis for the processing, or for direct marketing purposes.
  4. Right to erasure: You have the right to request the deletion of your personal data in certain circumstances, such as where it is no longer necessary for the Company to retain it.
  5. Right to data portability: You have the right to receive your personal data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format, and to request that it be transferred to another controller, where technically feasible.
  6. Right to withdraw consent: If the Company is processing your personal data based on your consent, you have the right to withdraw that consent at any time. Please note that this may affect the Company's ability to provide certain services to you.

You can exercise any of these rights by contacting the Company. The Company may ask you to verify your identity before responding to your request.

Your Rights under the CCPA

As a resident of California, you have certain rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) regarding your personal data. These rights include

  1. Right to notice: You have the right to be properly informed about the categories of personal data that are being collected and the purposes for which they are being used.
  2. Right to access/request: You have the right to request and obtain from the Company information about any disclosures of your personal data that have been made to third parties for their direct marketing purposes in the past 12 months.
  3. Right to opt-out of sale: You have the right to request that the Company does not sell your personal data to third parties. You can submit this request through the Company's "Do Not Sell My Personal Information" web page.
  4. Right to know: You have the right to request and obtain from the Company information about the personal data that has been collected, including categories of data, sources, business or commercial purposes, and categories of third parties with whom the data has been shared.
  5. Right to delete: You have the right to request the deletion of your personal data that has been collected in the past 12 months.
  6. Right to non-discrimination: The Company may not discriminate against you for exercising any of your rights under the CCPA, including by denying goods or services, charging different prices, or providing different levels of quality.

You can exercise your rights under the CCPA by contacting the Company via email, phone or by visiting the Company's "Do Not Sell My Personal Information" web page.
